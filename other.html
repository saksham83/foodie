Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  2. Finely chop the pancetta, having first removed any rind.
  3. Finely grate both cheeses and mix them together.
  4. Beat the eggs in a medium bowl, season with a little freshly grated black pepper and set everything aside.
  5. Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water, add the spaghetti and cook according to the pack instructions.
  6. Squash 2 peeled plump garlic cloves with the blade of a knife, just to bruise it.
  7. While the spaghetti is cooking, fry the pancetta with the garlic.
  8. Keep the heat hot but not too hot, so the pancetta fats render out without burning the garlic.
  9. Remove the garlic from the pan.
  10. Keep the heat under the pancetta on low when the pasta is ready.
  11. Drain the pasta well and add to the pancetta.
  12. Toss everything to combine, remove from the heat and add the cheese and egg mixture.
  13. Mix everything together quickly to ensure the egg doesnâ€™t scramble.
  14. Serve immediately with a little sprinkling of the remaining cheese and a grating of black pepper.
